Why email follows the contract's logic

Mercury rules the written and the sent word: contracts, messages, the small print and the pitch alike. An important email is a contract in miniature, an offer of your terms put into someone else's hands, which is why this page reuses the same traditional election as our contract timing rather than inventing a separate rule set: the Moon applying to Mercury or Jupiter, away from Mars and Saturn's harder contacts, with Mercury direct above all, the one non-negotiable condition.

What to avoid in 2027

The three Mercury retrogrades: Feb 10 to Mar 4, Jun 11 to Jul 5, and Oct 8 to 29, excluded from every date above. A message sent while Mercury is turned back is more likely to be misread, to land with the wrong person, or to need a second try. See the full Mercury retrograde 2027 guide.

Not every email needs this. Save it for the one you only want to send once: the pitch, the ask, the negotiation, the message you have rewritten four times because it matters. Routine correspondence can go out any day Mercury is direct.

Common questions

When is the best time to send an important email, by astrology?

The best days in 2027 have the Moon applying to Mercury or Jupiter with Mercury direct: January 13, March 24, April 23, July 11, and August 3 are the year's strongest, each rated Good on the Twelve Rooms Timing Scale. Mercury retrograde is excluded from every date, since Mercury rules written and sent words.

Why does Mercury retrograde matter for sending an email?

Mercury governs writing, sending, and being understood. Traditionally, messages sent while Mercury is retrograde are more likely to be misread, delayed, sent to the wrong person, or simply need a follow-up. It is not a hard rule, just the reason a genuinely important message is worth timing around it.

Is this the same timing as signing a contract?

Yes, by design. An important email and a contract are both Mercury's business, the word put into motion, so this page reuses the same traditional logic as our contract timing: the Moon applying to Mercury or Jupiter, Mercury direct. See the full doctrine on the electional astrology guide.

What kind of email counts as important enough to time?

The ask that matters: a pitch, a negotiation, a first outreach to someone you want to hear back from, a difficult message you only want to send once. Routine correspondence does not need this level of care; the tradition is for the message you want to land clean and be believed.
