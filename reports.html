Questions, answered

What makes these different from a computer-generated report?

They are written, not assembled. One cohesive reading from your exact birth data, with the themes connected across sections. I read every line myself before it ships, and if you have questions after you read it, message the shop and a human answers.

Do I need my exact birth time?

It matters more than most reports admit: the birth time sets your rising sign, Midheaven, and houses. Bring it if you can (birth certificates usually have it). Without it, the reading covers the planets fully and skips the houses rather than guessing.

Which house system do you use?

Whole-sign houses, the system Hellenistic astrology was built on, with your chart's sect weighed the old way. If you follow modern traditional astrologers, this is that language.
