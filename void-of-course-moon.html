What void of course actually means

The Moon moves faster than any other body, roughly thirteen degrees a day, and she spends about two and a half days crossing each sign. In that time she makes a series of exact aspects, trines, sextiles, squares, oppositions, conjunctions, to the other planets, each one a fresh connection. At some point in every sign, she makes her last exact aspect before leaving it, and from that moment until she actually crosses into the next sign, she is void of course: still moving, still changing degree, but forming no new aspects at all.

The traditional reading is simple. A beginning wants connection: a new plan, a signature, a launch, a first conversation, all want the Moon actively reaching toward something. A void Moon is reaching toward nothing. Astrologers have long held that whatever starts in the void tends to drift, stall, or quietly come to nothing, not from bad luck exactly, more like starting a conversation with someone who has already left the room.

What to do while she is void

Hold off on: signing something you want to hold, launching something you want to grow, a first date, a proposal, any decision you want to actually stick. Lean into: finishing what is already moving, tidying, resting, reflecting, routine tasks that do not need momentum behind them. The void is not empty time, it is unattached time, better suited to closing loops than opening new ones.

Common questions

What does void of course moon mean?

The Moon is void of course after she makes her last major (Ptolemaic) aspect to another planet while in a sign, and before she crosses into the next sign. During that gap she is forming no new connections, so beginnings started then classically tend to drift or come to nothing.

Is void of course moon bad?

No, it is a pause, not a curse. It is a poor time to begin something you want to go somewhere: launch, sign, propose, decide. It is a good time to rest, tidy, reflect, and finish what is already underway. The old counsel is to work with the pause rather than against it.

How long does void of course moon last?
